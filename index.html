About Us


IfyTech Global was founded by Ifeyinwa Maryann Okonkwo, a highly skilled professional with years of experience working with diverse businesses, from law firms and interior companies to startups and service-driven brands.
At IfyTech Global, we specialize in providing cutting-edge solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of businesses across various industries.

With a strong foundation in lead generation, telemarketing, branding, web development (HTML, CSS, PHP, MySQL, JavaScript), Cloud Computing, DevOps engineering, and administrative efficiency, we have helped both startups and established companies achieve their goals. Our expertise spans multiple sectors, including law firms, interior design, technology, and retail, ensuring that we bring diverse insights to every project.

Our goal is to become your trusted partner for branding, lead generation, web development, Cloud solutions and business growth strategies. We combine creativity, technical skills, and strategic thinking to help you achieve your business goals.
Whether you’re a startup, SME, or large enterprise, we are here to elevate your brand and streamline your operations.
